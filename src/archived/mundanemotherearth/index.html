Mundane Mother Earth 28/01/2022 //Go back//

The earth may very well be the oldest and prettiest model known to mankind, shes nurtured us with her views, inspired art and encouraged us to explore for the sheer reason of "I want to see whats out there." And yet, alot of the time I find that people ignore parts of our little shared globe because they're not photogenic or bleak and uninspiring. I really disagree with this viewpoint, theres so much to be found often in the places that we dont expect or look hard enough at, the mundane can be just as pretty if not prettier in its own unique way then famous landscapes. This page is my attempt to afirm that fact by regularly attaching photos of things I think are beautiful, but aren't neccisarily what we associate with the term traditionally.
